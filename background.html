Education & Background

Education

I'm finishing my Master's in Electrical Engineering, with a focus on Computer Systems, at Stanford, advised by David Mazières. Starting Fall 2026, I'll pursue a PhD at Boston University, advised by Kinan Dak Albab as part of the SPACE group.

I completed my BS in Electrical Engineering, summa cum laude with a 4.0 GPA, from Arizona State University.

Research Interests

My research focuses on systems security, particularly lightweight fault isolation, WebAssembly sandboxing, RISC-V systems, and embedded systems security. I am also interested in usable security and in combining HCI with systems research to build security mechanisms that are not only technically strong, but also practical and usable for real people.
